About

I was born in Northwest Ohio in November of 2004. When I was very young my mom was charged with a drug based crime, and I was put in foster care. After a few years my grandma obtained custody and I was back in my home town of Defiance. My grandma passed away of a heart attack when I was in sixth grade, and because she had custody over me at the time, I was put into foster care again. After a year, my mom finally regained custody and I came back to live with her. Not long after, we moved to Napoleon, where I currently live. I am currently enrolled at Four County Career Center in Mr. Geiger's computer programming lab.
