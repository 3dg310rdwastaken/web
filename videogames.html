• #10. Among Us

    While I know Among Us has taken on a bit of a meme role in recent times, the game does have some merit to it. Among Us is a simple game of Mafia, but with a twist, instead of simply needing to find the Impostors, the Crewmates can also win by completing short minigames called "tasks". The Impostors, too, have a trick up their sleeve however. The Impostors are able to sabotage important functions such as lights or comms.
